Target Audience

The target audience for this website is every business owner in the area of Rexburg that wants to market their brands and reach more people, connect with other owners, and engage with the community. This website also serve the community of Rexburg and all those people interested in the events taking place in the area.

Persona 1:

Fictional Name:

Lincoln Sorenson

Job Title:

Business Owner

Demographics:

  • 36 years old
  • Married
  • 4 children
  • Bachelors Degree in Business

Goals and Tasks:

He is determined and one of his concerns is maintaining quality in his business.
Spends his work time:

  • Writing emails and making calls.
  • Talking to customers over the phone and in person.
  • Training employees.

Environment:

He is comfortable using a computer and refers to himself as an intermediate Internet user. Although he prefers a computer screen, he uses his phone to verify information online.
