Target Audience

The target audience for this website is every business owner in the area of Rexburg that wants to market their brands and reach more people, connect with other owners, and engage with the community.

This website also serve the community of Rexburg and all those people interested in the events taking place in the area.

Persona 1:

Fictional Name:

Lincoln Sorenson

Job Title:

Business Owner

Demographics:

  • 36 years old
  • Married
  • 4 children
  • Bachelors Degree in Business

Goals and Tasks:

He is determined and one of his concerns is maintaining quality in his business.
Spends his work time:

  • Writing emails and making calls.
  • Talking to customers over the phone and in person.
  • Training employees.

Environment:

He is comfortable using a computer and refers to himself as an intermediate Internet user. Although he prefers a computer screen, he uses his phone to verify information online.

She uses the computer in a daily basis and refers to herself as intermediate user. She navigate most websites through her mobile devices (tablet and phone).

Scenarios:

  • She wants "Dinner-At-Grandma's" to be outside this time she needs to check the weather to see if it will be a comfortable evening outside.
  • With her morning walks, Barbra checks the weather to decide what would be the most comfortable to wear in that temperature.
  • She is planning a play-day with her grandchildren and needs to know what the weather is going to be like to plan accordingly.
  • She and her kids are planning a 3 day get-together and she wants to make it the best 3 days. She checks the weather to see what the best 3 consecutive days would be to plan their activity.
  • Her grandchildren want to go sledding, and she wants to take them. She needs to figure out which day(s) would have the safest snow for sledding.

Scenarios:

  • Lincoln has a scheduled tour starting in about 15 minutes, he needs to see the weather to help him be aware of possible trail conditions so he can drive safer, and he can let the tour group know to prepare for the weather during the tour.
  • He and a few friends are planning to go on a mountain bike trail, to make sure that everyone is as safe as possible Lincoln can look at the weather to be aware of trail conditions and/or prepare for the weather conditions during their trail riding.
  • To save money, he rides his bike to school. Before he leaves the house each time, he checks the weather to see if he should bring his coat, or to ride slower and leave early in case of ice.
  • He and his wife planned a date night going to have a picnic dinner and watch a movie outside. He needs to check the weather to see if they need to bring coats or move their picnic/movie undercover.
  • Winter is coming and his car still doesn't have winter tires, he needs to know which day would be the safest day to take his car in to get winter tires on.
